Overview

Purpose

I love board games. I play board games on a weekly basis. I own over a 150 board/card games. I have a big circle of friends that play games with me. Any chance that we get we share our opinions on the different games that we play. This has led to a lot of people purchasing games that we recommended and giving feedback about their experience. So many people enjoy playing games. Sometimes it is hard to sort through the thousands of different games that you can find online. Having a convenient place to go for recommendations will help speed fun to future game nights. Those that start exploring will then want to return the favor spreading the joy of games for all.

Audience

Everyone looking for a recommendation is welcome here. Games are meant to be shared so that we might all have fun together. I want to reach as many people as I can. I want to cultivate somewhere where everyone can share together. I have had the pleasure of recommending games to people from ages 12-80 and wish to continue doing so. Board Game Reviewers aims to bring all together in fun and adventure. I hope others will want to share their own recommendations.
