Game of the Month

Talisman is a RPG board game where you take control of one of a variety of different characters and attempt to beat everyone else to the crown of power. To progress you will have to overcome events, visit shops, and take down a variety of creatures both mortal and not so mortal. As you progress your strength in both mind and body will increase until you are strong enough to challenge the trials of the inner circle. Games can take anywhere from 2-10 hours depending on who are you playing with, so it is not for the faint of heart. Do you have what it takes to become the greatest in all the land?
