Overview

Purpose

We want to build an online presence to reach a broader audience and showcase the fun to be had. In addition, to providing information, in the form of the adventure one would take and the affordable pricing, we want to ensure that people can share their own experiences as they adventure forth.

Audience

Everyone that is a lover of having fun and searching for an adventure is welcome here. Life has a tendency to get everyone down and people need an escape. Dry Oar's grand adventure offers a variety of options for all walks of life: Short Excursions, Extreme Sporters, and longer trips.
